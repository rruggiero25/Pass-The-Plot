# Privacy Policy

**App:** Pass The Plot  
**Effective Date:** March 3, 2026

---

## Overview

Pass The Plot is a local multiplayer party game where players pass prompts and drawings between devices. Your privacy matters to us. This policy explains what data the app collects, how it is used, and your rights as a user.

---

## 1. Information We Collect

### 1.1 Information You Provide

- **Display Name** — A nickname you choose before joining or hosting a session. It is visible only to players in the same local game session.
- **Game Content** — Text prompts and drawings you create during gameplay. This content is shared only with players connected to the same session.

### 1.2 Information Collected Automatically

Pass The Plot operates entirely on-device and over local peer-to-peer connections. We do not operate servers or collect usage data. The only data stored automatically is a randomly generated device UUID, created locally and used solely to maintain your identity within a session.

---

## 2. How We Use Your Information

Pass The Plot uses the information described above exclusively to:

- Display your chosen name to other players in a local session.
- Facilitate real-time gameplay (passing prompts, drawings, and story chains between connected devices).
- Identify your device within a session for reconnection purposes.

We do not use your information for advertising, profiling, or any purpose beyond enabling the game.

---

## 3. Data Sharing and Disclosure

Pass The Plot does not share your data with third parties. All gameplay data — names, prompts, and drawings — is transmitted directly between players' devices over a local Wi-Fi or Bluetooth network using Apple's MultipeerConnectivity framework. No data is transmitted to the developers or any external server.

The only exception is if you choose to use the **Share** button on the Reveal screen, which invokes iOS's native share sheet. In that case, the content you share is handled by the app or service you choose (e.g. Photos, Messages), subject to its own privacy policy.

---

## 4. Data Storage and Retention

All gameplay data is held in memory only and is cleared when the session ends or the app is closed. The only data persisted on your device is:

- A randomly generated **UUID** (stored in UserDefaults) used to identify your device across sessions. It contains no personal information and can be reset by deleting and reinstalling the app.
- The **last lobby code** and host peer identifier, stored temporarily to support automatic reconnection if you are disconnected. This is cleared when you leave a lobby.

---

## 5. Children's Privacy

Pass The Plot does not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. The app does not require account registration or any form of personal identification. If you are a parent or guardian and believe your child has submitted personal information through the app, please contact us and we will assist you.

---

## 6. Security

Pass The Plot uses Apple's MultipeerConnectivity framework with encryption required (`MCEncryptionPreference.required`) for all peer-to-peer communication. No data leaves your local network.

---

## 7. Your Rights

Since Pass The Plot does not collect or store personal data on external servers, most data subject rights (access, deletion, portability) are satisfied automatically — your data simply never leaves your device. You may:

- Delete the locally stored UUID by uninstalling the app.
- Contact us at any time with questions or requests regarding your privacy.

---

## 8. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. When we do, we will revise the Effective Date at the top of this document. We encourage you to review this policy periodically. Continued use of Pass The Plot after changes are posted constitutes your acceptance of the updated policy.

---

## 9. Contact

If you have any questions or concerns regarding this Privacy Policy, you can reach us through the **App Store review page** for Pass The Plot.
